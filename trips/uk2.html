Uttarakhand 8-Day Trip Itinerary

Traveling Together: Aayush, Mandar, Wasim, Ninad

From Noida, 30 Sept – 7 Oct 2025

Focus: Less travel, more exploration, budget-friendly food options included

Departure: 07:00 AM | Arrival: 02:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~260 km / 6–7 hrs

Activity: Check-in, relax, riverside walk, photography

Stay: Onyx Hotel, Corbett

Food Suggestions: Breakfast on-the-way at Murthal dhabas (~₹150–200 per person), Lunch at Ram Nagar local restaurant (~₹200–250 per person)

Note / Optional: Corbett Falls (~1 hr), Garjia Temple (~30–45 min), Jim Corbett Museum (~1 hr morning)

Departure: 06:30 AM | Arrival: 03:30 PM

Distance & Duration: ~160 km / 5–6 hrs

Activity: Kaichi Dham darshan (~1–2 hrs), Sunset Point, stroll tea gardens

Stay: Himalaya Darshan / Chevron Eco Lodge, Kausani

Parking Paid ₹50–100

Food Suggestions: Breakfast at Corbett hotel, Lunch en route at Bageshwar local dhaba (~₹150 per person), Evening snack: Kausani local tea/fast food (~₹50–100)

Note / Optional: Baijnath Temple (~45 min), Anasakti Ashram (~30 min), Kausani Tea Gardens photography (~45 min)

Departure: 07:00 AM | Arrival: 03:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~190 km / 7–8 hrs

Activity: Check-in, relax, evening mountain views

Stay: The Tattva Resort / Blue Poppy, Auli or GMVN Joshimath

Food Suggestions: Breakfast at hotel, Lunch en route at Almora dhaba (~₹150 per person), Evening snack at Auli resort (~₹100)

Note / Optional: Ranikhet detour (~1 hr extra), Bageshwar stop (~30–45 min), Saraswati Mandir visit (~30 min)

Activity: Ropeway to Auli Top, Gorson Bugyal trek, photography, relax

Stay: Same as Day 3

Parking Ropeway Base ₹100/day

Food Suggestions: Breakfast at hotel, Lunch at Auli resort (~₹250–300 per person), Evening snacks at local café (~₹100)

Note / Optional: Extended Gorson Bugyal trek (~2–3 hrs), Auli Artificial Lake visit

Departure: 06:30 AM | Arrival: 07:30 PM

Distance & Duration: ~45 km one-way (~2–3 hrs each)

Activity: Badrinath darshan, Mana Village, Vyas Gufa

Stay: GMVN Joshimath / Snow Crest Inn

Parking ₹100–150

Food Suggestions: Breakfast at hotel, Lunch near Badrinath (~₹200), Evening tea/snacks in Mana Village (~₹50–100)

Note / Optional: Vasudhara Falls trek (~2–3 hrs), Ganesh Gufa (~30 min)

Departure: 06:00 AM | Arrival: 02:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~250 km / 8–9 hrs

Evening Activity: Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat

Stay: Zostel / Live Free Hostel or Aloha on the Ganges

Food Suggestions: Breakfast at Joshimath hotel, Lunch on route in Haridwar (~₹150 per person), Evening snack at Ghat (~₹50)

Note / Optional: Riverside walk in Rishikesh (~30–45 min)

Activities: River rafting (~₹500–700 pp), Bungee Jump (~₹3,500 pp, optional), Laxman Jhula, Beatles Café

Stay: Same as Day 6

Food Suggestions: Breakfast at hotel, Lunch at Tapovan café (~₹200–250), Evening snacks (~₹100)

Note / Optional: Neer Garh Waterfall trek (~30–45 min), evening café hopping in Tapovan

Departure: 07:00 AM | Arrival: 12:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~250 km / 5–6 hrs

Food Suggestions: Breakfast at Rishikesh, Lunch en route at Meerut / Ghaziabad (~₹150 per person)

Note / Optional: Quick stop at Haridwar for Ganga Aarti

Trip ends at Noida

Essential Packing List

Clothing
  • Full-sleeve T-shirts / Shirts – 4–5
  • Light sweater / fleece – 1–2
  • Warm jacket / windcheater – 1
  • Thermal innerwear – 1–2 sets
  • Comfortable trekking pants – 2–3
  • Shorts / casual pants – 1–2
  • Nightwear – 1–2
  • Rain jacket / poncho – 1 per person
Footwear & Accessories
  • Sturdy walking shoes / trekking shoes
  • Sandals / flip-flops
  • Socks – 6–7 pairs
  • Gloves, cap, woolen hat
  • Sunglasses, sunscreen, lip balm
  • Small backpack / daypack
  • Reusable water bottle
Electronics & Health
  • Mobile charger, power bank
  • Camera / GoPro
  • Basic first-aid kit & medicines
  • Travel documents & ID proofs
  • Snacks for travel (~₹100 per person per day optional)
