Uttarakhand 8-Day Trip Itinerary (30 Sept – 7 Oct 2025)

Day 1: 30 Sept (Tue) – Noida → Jim Corbett

Departure: 07:00 AM | Arrival: 02:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~260 km / 6–7 hrs

Activity: Check-in, relax, riverside walk, photography

Stay: Onyx Hotel, Corbett

Day 2: 1 Oct (Wed) – Corbett → Kaichi Dham → Kausani

Departure: 06:30 AM | Arrival: 03:30 PM

Distance & Duration: ~160 km / 5–6 hrs

Activity: Kaichi Dham darshan (~1–2 hrs), Sunset Point, stroll tea gardens

Stay: Himalaya Darshan / Chevron Eco Lodge, Kausani

Parking: Paid ₹50–100

Day 3: 2 Oct (Thu) – Kausani → Joshimath / Auli

Departure: 07:00 AM | Arrival: 03:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~190 km / 7–8 hrs

Activity: Check-in, relax, evening mountain views

Stay: The Tattva Resort / Blue Poppy, Auli or GMVN Joshimath

Day 4: 3 Oct (Fri) – Full Day Auli Exploration

Activity: Ropeway to Auli Top (₹1,000–1,200 pp), trek to Gorson Bugyal, photography, relax

Evening: Resort chill, dinner with mountain view

Stay: Same as Day 3

Parking: Ropeway base ₹100/day

Day 5: 4 Oct (Sat) – Auli → Badrinath → Mana Village → Joshimath

Departure: 06:30 AM | Arrival: 07:30 PM

Distance & Duration: ~45 km one-way (~2–3 hrs each)

Activity: Badrinath darshan, Mana Village, Vyas Gufa, optional trek to Vasudhara Falls

Stay: GMVN Joshimath / Snow Crest Inn

Parking: ~₹100–150

Day 6: 5 Oct (Sun) – Joshimath → Rishikesh

Departure: 06:00 AM | Arrival: 02:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~250 km / 8–9 hrs

Evening Activity: Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat

Stay: Zostel / Live Free Hostel (budget) or Aloha on the Ganges (comfortable)

Parking: Triveni Ghat ~₹50/hr

Day 7: 6 Oct (Mon) – Rishikesh Adventure & Exploration

Activities: River rafting (₹500–700 pp), Bungee Jump (₹3,500 pp, optional), Laxman Jhula, Beatles Café

Stay: Same as Day 6

Day 8: 7 Oct (Tue) – Rishikesh → Noida

Departure: 07:00 AM | Arrival: 12:00 PM

Distance & Duration: ~250 km / 5–6 hrs

Optional: Quick stop at Haridwar for Ganga Aarti

Trip ends at Noida

Optional / Additional Nearby Places

Day 1 – Jim Corbett
  • Corbett Falls – Afternoon after check-in (~45–60 min)
  • Garjia Temple – Sunset (~30–45 min)
  • Jim Corbett Museum – Morning if extra time (~1 hr)
Day 2 – Kaichi Dham / Kausani
  • Baijnath Temple – Morning on the way (~45–60 min)
  • Anasakti Ashram – Late afternoon (~30–45 min)
  • Kausani Tea Gardens – Evening / sunset photography (~45–60 min)
Day 3 – Kausani → Joshimath / Auli
  • Ranikhet – Morning optional detour (~1 hr, adds ~2 hrs travel)
  • Bageshwar – Afternoon tea/lunch stop (~30–45 min)
  • Saraswati Mandir / Sunset Point – Quick evening stop (~30 min)

Essential Packing List

Clothing
  • Full-sleeve T-shirts / Shirts – 4–5
  • Light sweater / fleece – 1–2
  • Warm jacket / windcheater – 1
  • Thermal innerwear – 1–2 sets (for high altitude)
  • Comfortable trekking pants – 2–3
  • Shorts / casual pants – 1–2
  • Nightwear – 1–2
  • Rain jacket / poncho – 1 per person
Footwear & Accessories
  • Sturdy walking shoes / trekking shoes
  • Sandals / flip-flops
  • Socks (preferably wool) – 5–6 pairs
  • Sunglasses & cap
  • Gloves & woolen cap (for Auli/Joshimath)
  • Scarf / muffler
  • Backpack / daypack
  • Water bottle (1–2 L)
Toiletries & Gadgets
  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, soap, shampoo, towel
  • Sunscreen SPF 30+
  • Lip balm
  • Hand sanitizer & wet wipes
  • Prescription medicines & basic first-aid kit
  • Mobile phones + chargers + power bank
  • Camera / GoPro
  • Headlamp / flashlight (optional)
Documents & Miscellaneous
  • ID proof (Aadhaar / Passport)
  • Train tickets / PNR
  • Hotel booking confirmations
  • Driving license & car documents
  • Emergency contacts
  • Snacks & energy bars
  • Plastic bags for wet/dirty clothes
  • Trekking poles (optional)
Season-Specific Tips
  • Daytime in plains (Noida/Corbett/Rishikesh): light clothing, mornings/evenings slightly chilly
  • Hill stations (Kausani, Joshimath, Auli): layering recommended (t-shirt → fleece → jacket)
  • High altitude (Auli, Badrinath): temperatures can drop to 5–10°C, carry gloves, thermals, woolens
  • Sun protection: sunglasses, sunscreen, and caps needed at high altitude
