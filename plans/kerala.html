Day 1: Kochi Arrival & Sightseeing

Arrive in Kochi. Visit Fort Kochi, Mattancherry Palace, Jewish Synagogue, and Chinese fishing nets. Try Kerala-style seafood for dinner. Overnight in Kochi.

Day 2: Kochi ‚Äì Munnar

Drive to Munnar (approx. 4-5 hrs). En route visit waterfalls and tea plantations. Evening leisure walk in Munnar town. Try local tea and snacks. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 3: Munnar Hill Tour

Explore Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and Top Station. Visit a tea museum. Adventure options: trekking, jeep safari. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 4: Munnar ‚Äì Thekkady

Drive to Thekkady. Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary with boating. Explore spice plantations and attend Kathakali dance or Kalaripayattu martial arts show. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 5: Thekkady ‚Äì Alleppey (Houseboat)

Drive to Alleppey. Board a traditional Kerala houseboat for a backwater cruise. Enjoy Kerala meals on board. Overnight stay in the houseboat.

Day 6: Alleppey ‚Äì Kovalam

Drive to Kovalam. Relax on Lighthouse Beach, Hawah Beach, and Samudra Beach. Enjoy Ayurvedic massage. Try Malabar cuisine for dinner. Overnight in Kovalam.

Day 7: Kovalam ‚Äì Trivandrum Departure

Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, and shopping markets in Trivandrum before departure. End of Kerala trip.
