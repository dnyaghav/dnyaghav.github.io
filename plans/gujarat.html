Day 1: Ahmedabad Arrival & Heritage Tour

Arrive in Ahmedabad. Visit Sabarmati Ashram, Jama Masjid, Sidi Saiyyed Mosque, and Kankaria Lake. Taste local snacks like Dhokla and Fafda. Overnight in Ahmedabad.

Day 2: Ahmedabad ‚Äì Little Rann of Kutch

Drive to Little Rann of Kutch. Enjoy jeep safari to spot wild asses, desert foxes, and migratory birds. Try local Kutchi thali. Overnight stay near Rann.

Day 3: Rann of Kutch ‚Äì White Desert

Morning excursion to White Desert. Witness sunrise and sunset over salt flats. Explore nearby Hodka village for handicrafts. Enjoy cultural dance in the evening. Overnight stay in Bhuj.

Day 4: Bhuj & Mandvi

Visit Aina Mahal, Prag Mahal, and Mandvi beach. Try Kutchi food like Bajra Roti with garlic chutney. Enjoy sunset at Mandvi beach. Overnight in Bhuj.

Day 5: Dwarka Pilgrimage

Drive to Dwarka. Visit Dwarkadhish Temple, Bet Dwarka, and Nageshwar Jyotirlinga. Evening aarti at Dwarkadhish temple. Overnight in Dwarka.

Day 6: Somnath & Gir National Park

Morning visit Somnath Temple (Jyotirlinga). Then proceed to Gir National Park. Evening jungle safari to spot Asiatic Lions. Overnight near Gir.

Day 7: Departure from Rajkot/Ahmedabad

Drive back to Rajkot or Ahmedabad for departure. If time permits, enjoy last-minute shopping for Bandhani sarees, handicrafts, and Gujarati snacks.
