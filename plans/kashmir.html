Day 1: Srinagar Arrival & Local Sightseeing

Arrive at Srinagar International Airport. Visit Mughal Gardens (Nishat, Shalimar, Pari Mahal), enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake, and taste Kashmiri Kehwa & fruit chaat. Overnight in Srinagar hotel.

Day 2: Sonmarg Day Tour

Drive (2 hrs) to Sonmarg. Explore snowy glaciers, pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier, sledding, rafting, and enjoy local snacks. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day 3: Doodhpathri Day Tour

Drive to Doodhpathri (1hr 50 min). Known for green meadows & streams at 9,000 ft. Relaxing walks, pony rides, and picnic spots. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day 4: Srinagar to Gulmarg

Drive to Gulmarg (2 hrs). Take Gondola ride (world‚Äôs 2nd highest cable car). Enjoy skiing, snowboarding (winter) or ATV/pony rides (summer). Return to Srinagar hotel.

Day 5: Gulmarg to Pahalgam

Drive (3 hrs) to Pahalgam. Visit Lidder River, Mini-Switzerland, Baisaran, and waterfalls. Try hiking, pony rides, and river rafting. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 6: Pahalgam to Srinagar (Houseboat Stay)

Visit Betaab Valley & Aru Valley. Picnic by Lidder River. Return to Srinagar & spend night on a houseboat at Nigeen Lake.

Day 7: Departure

Final breakfast. Visit Dal Lake or shop for Pashmina, carpets, and handicrafts. Driver drops at Srinagar Airport for departure.
